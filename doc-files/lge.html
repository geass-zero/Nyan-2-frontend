What is Nyan.finance?

Nyan.finance is a community driven, decentralized, hedgefunding project.
Participants can create investment proposals that benefits all holders, cast their vote on other proposals to get it to pass, or put up a governance vote for steering the project's mid- to long-term vision and execution plan.

What is an LGE and why is Nyan.finance having one?

Nyan.finance is currently transitioning from the V1 tokenomics to a more robust, flexible and improved V2 system. In order to make sure the V2 hedgefund has enough liquidity to start operating from the start with accceptable yielding, a proposal to organize a Liquid Generation Event has been put up for voting, and passed with 3204votes. 
 
The Liquid Generation Event aims to gather enough liquidity in 4 days to guarantee a smooth V1 to V2 swap, right after the closing. 

Current (and new) holders of the NYAN v1 token are being encouraged to take part in the LGE by receiving benefits listed below:
 
  • LGE participants will be joining the pool early and at a reduced cost of .1ETH per NYAN staked
  • LGE participants will also receive a mining boost in the form of an NFT, after the LGE concludes. The details for this NFT are as follows:
    • 0-3 ETH: no mining boost
    • 3-6 ETH: 5% mining boost
    • 6-18 ETH: 7.5% mining boost
    • 18-36 ETH: 12.5% mining boost
    • 36+ ETH: 20% mining boost

 

I have V1 NYAN and I want to participate. Now what do I do?

In order to participate:

  • Make sure you're using a web3 (Metamask/WalletConnect) enabled browser, preferably on desktop
  • Go to https://v2.nyan.finance
  • Click on the menu item called "LGE" in the top right corner
  • Input the amount of NYAN you wish to stake in the input field
  • Click on "Swap!"
  • Sign your transaction and await confirmation
  • Thats it! You're now part of our V2 Hedgefund!

 

After the LGE has been concluded we will update everybody involved using our official channels (Telegram, Twitter). Visit us and stay tuned for our next steps into the DeFi Hedgefund space!
